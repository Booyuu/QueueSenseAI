Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the wait time estimation?

Our wait time is calculated using real-time data combined with historical patterns via our n8n logic. We usually estimate within a 2-3 minute margin of error.

Does it cover all canteens in RP?

Currently, we fully support South Canteen and North Canteen, including all halal and non-halal stalls. Lawn canteen integration is coming soon!

Do I need an app to use this?

No extra apps required! As long as you have Telegram installed on your phone or laptop, simply search for our bot and start chatting.
